About Me


Hello! My name is Felicia Sunanto and I was born and raised right here in Tucson, AZ where everything wants to poke or sting you. I really love animals, plants, and nature so I decided to attend the University of Arizona and received a bachelor's degree in natural resources with a focus on wildlife management and conservation (wordy I know). I currently work for the USDA Forest Service as a wild-land firefighter but I am sort of a trick of all trades. I also work for developed recreation and maintain all of the picnic sites, campgrounds, and disperse campgrounds. Familiar places you may know of are Mount Lemmon, Sabino Canyon, and Redington Pass. Lastly, I have done work in rangeland management and invasive species removal which involved a lot of back-country hiking and off-roading in secluded areas to collect vegetative field data.

Aside from that passion, I really love everything about the video game and technology industry. I am an avid gamer and that's probably what you'll find me doing on my free time. One of my favorite gigs was working as a social media representative for a video card company called "XFX". My duty was to literally talk about anything related to video games every day and it was awesome! Which brings me here, as to why I am attending the program! I still have that strong "itch" to see if I could make a career path into the industry.

What's this? Still reading? Okay! I have a precious black cat with yellow golden eyes, her name is Okami (yes, named after the video game), and she loves to play fetch..."yaaaaas". My favorite anime film director is Hayao Miazaki/Studio Ghibli. No one can beat me at Mariokart 64. I built my own PC, there is just so much satisfaction doing it yourself! I enjoy painting and baking treats. I have a bracelet that is permanently stuck on my wrist since elementary school. That is all.
